New energy vehicles VS gasoline vehicles

New energy vehicles are a very popular type of car today. He is very famous because of green environmental protection and low maintenance costs. Of course, there are many types of new energy vehicles, but in the region's automobile industry is increasingly perfect today, electric vehicles have become the most important part of new energy vehicles. So let's look at electric cars versus gasoline cars
Zero to 100 acceleration comparison

New energy vehicles are divided into the following types

  • electric vehicle
    • Battery Electric Vehicles (BEVs)
      • It uses electricity stored in a battery to power a motor and turn the wheels
      • It needs to be charged using electricity from the grid
      • It only runs on batteries
    • Hybrid Electric Vehicles (HEVs)
      • It has both an electric motor and an engine
      • The engine can charge the battery
      • The motor and engine switch between each other while driving
      • You only need to refuel, you don't need to recharge
    • Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEVs)
      • It has both an electric motor and an engine
      • The motor and engine can be controlled independently or work together
      • The engine cannot charge the battery
      • It needs to be refueled and recharged
  • Fuel-cell Electric Vehicles (FCEVs)
      • You need to fill it with hydrogen or methanol
      • The chemical energy of a fuel is changed into electrical energy by means of an electrochemical reaction
      • Basically no pollution emissions
      • Not common, the price is relatively high
